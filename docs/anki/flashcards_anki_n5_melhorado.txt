〜くなる	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#990000;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Expressão</span> <b>Ficar/Tornar-se</b> — com ADJETIVO<br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Regra:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">ADJETIVO (forma く/に) + なる<br>い形容詞: 高<b>く</b>なる (高い→高く)<br>な形容詞: 静か<b>に</b>なる (静か→静かに)</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">物価</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">高く</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">なった</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Os preços ficaram caros</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">部屋</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">静かに</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">なった</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">O quarto ficou silencioso</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> い形容詞 → く+なる (高い→高くなる). な形容詞 → に+なる (静か→静かになる). に vs く é a diferença!</div></div>
〜があります	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#990000;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Existência</span> <b>Existir / Ter</b> — coisas inanimadas, plantas<br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Estrutura:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">LUGAR に COISA が あります<br><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">机の上</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">本</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">あります</span><br><span style="font-size:11px;color:#555;">MESA-SOBRE | EM | LIVRO | FOCO | EXISTE</span></span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">公園</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">ベンチ</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">あります</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Há/tem um banco no parque</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">冷蔵庫</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">あります</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">か</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">O que tem na geladeira?</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> あります = objetos, plantas, coisas inanimadas. います = pessoas, animais. LUGARに + COISAが + あります.</div></div>
〜がいます	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#990000;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Existência</span> <b>Existir / Ter</b> — pessoas, animais (seres vivos)<br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Estrutura:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">LUGAR に SER-VIVO が います<br><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">公園</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">子供</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">います</span><br><span style="font-size:11px;color:#555;">PARQUE | EM | CRIANÇAS | FOCO | EXISTEM</span></span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">部屋</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">猫</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">います</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Há/tem um gato no quarto</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#003d80;font-weight:bold;">彼</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">は</span><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">家</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">います</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Ele está em casa</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> います = pessoas, animais, seres vivos. あります = objetos, plantas. LUGARに + SERが + います.</div></div>
前 (まえ) ・ 後ろ (うしろ) ・ 中 (なか)	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#b35900;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Locução de posição</span> <b>Na frente ・ Atrás ・ Dentro</b> — substantivos de localização<br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Estrutura:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">SUBSTANTIVO + の + POSIÇÃO<br><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">駅</span>の<span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">前</span> = em frente à estação<br><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">家</span>の<span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">後ろ</span> = atrás de casa<br><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">箱</span>の<span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">中</span> = dentro da caixa</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">駅の前</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">で</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">会いましょう</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Vamos nos encontrar em frente à estação</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">箱の中</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">あります</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">か</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">O que tem dentro da caixa?</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> Substantivos de posição usam の: Xの前, Xの後ろ, Xの中, Xの上, Xの下, Xの隣, Xの近く.</div></div>
何か (なにか)	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#005a1e;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Pronome indefinido</span> <b>Algo</b>  <span style="color:#888;">(alguma coisa)</span><br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 何 (なに) + か:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">何 = "que/qual"  +  か = partícula de pergunta<br>何か = pergunta indefinida → "algo"</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何か</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">を</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">食べました</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">か</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Comeu alguma coisa?</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何か</span><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">問題</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">あります</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">か</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Tem algum problema?</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> 何か = afirmação: 'algo'. 何か〜か = pergunta: 'algo?'. 何も + negativo = 'nada'.</div></div>
何も + negativo (なにも)	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#005a1e;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Pronome indefinido neg.</span> <b>Nada</b> — sempre com verbo negativo<br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Regra:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">何も + VERBO NEGATIVO = nada<br><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何も</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">ない</span> = não tem nada / não há nada</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何も</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">見ませんでした</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Não vi nada</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何も</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">食べたくない</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Não quero comer nada</span></div><div style="background:#ffebee;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">❌ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">何も</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">食べた</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">❌ 何も SEMPRE com verbo NEGATIVO!</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> 何も só funciona com verbo negativo! 何もない = não há nada. 何も食べない = não comer nada.</div></div>
まだ〜ていません	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#990000;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Expressão</span> <b>Ainda não fiz...</b><br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Estrutura:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">まだ + FORMA TE + いません<br><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">まだ</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">宿題</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">を</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">していません</span> = Ainda não fiz a lição</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">まだ</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">昼ご飯</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">を</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">食べていません</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Ainda não almocei</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#b35900;font-weight:bold;">まだ</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">読んでいません</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Ainda não li</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> まだ = ainda. 〜ていません = forma TE negativa polida. していません = não fiz (de する).</div></div>
〜に行く (いく)	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#990000;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Expressão de propósito</span> <b>Ir para [fazer algo]</b><br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Estrutura:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">SUBSTANTIVO (ação) + に + 行く<br><span style="color:#003d80;font-weight:bold;">私</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">は</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">買い物</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">行きます</span> = Vou às compras</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">勉強</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">行く</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Ir estudar</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">映画</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">を</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">見に</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">行く</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Ir ver filme (VERBO + に + 行く)</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> Substantivo de ação + に: 買い物に行く. Verbo (forma ます sem ます) + に: 食べに行く = ir comer.</div></div>
〜に来る (くる)	<div style="color:#222;font-size:15px;"><span style="background:#990000;color:white;padding:3px 10px;border-radius:3px;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;">Expressão de propósito</span> <b>Vir para [fazer algo]</b><br><br><div style="background:#fff;padding:10px;border-radius:5px;margin:8px 0;"><b style="font-size:14px;">📊 Estrutura:</b><br><span style="font-size:14px;">SUBSTANTIVO (ação) + に + 来る<br><span style="color:#003d80;font-weight:bold;">友達</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">が</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">遊び</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">来ました</span> = O amigo veio para se divertir</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">手伝い</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">来た</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Vim ajudar</span></div><div style="background:#e8f5e9;padding:6px 10px;border-radius:4px;margin:4px 0;font-size:14px;">✅ <b><span style="color:#003d80;font-weight:bold;">日本</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">へ</span><span style="color:#005a1e;font-weight:bold;">勉強</span><span style="color:#6a0080;font-weight:bold;">に</span><span style="color:#990000;font-weight:bold;">来ました</span></b><br><span style="font-size:12px;color:#333;">Vim ao Japão para estudar</span></div><div style="background:#fff3cd;padding:8px;border-radius:4px;margin:6px 0;font-size:13px;color:#333;"><b>💡</b> Mesmo padrão que 〜に行く, só troca 行く por 来る. Propósito + に + movimento (行く/来る/帰る).</div></div>
